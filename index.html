Skyflow.io

Connect Enterprise Clouds seemlessly

  • Connect your Cloud services from multiple providers & APIs (Salesforce, SAP, Microsoft, IBM, HP, Oracle…)
  • Route data, external events and information flows generated by IoT and customer facing apps.
  • Structure the connections between external events (web, forms, partners, clients, IoT) and internal “business” clouds

Skyflow.io is the maestro for all your Clouds

Knowledge

  • Corporate knowledge is already secured in terms of Data (CRM, ERP, Marketing databases, HR, custom business apps…)
  • Skyflow.io allows users to see and maintain the complementary part of your assets: Links and flows.

From external events to Enterprise value

  • Transform your websites, forms, apps, and IoT data into business value
  • Skyflow lets users centralize flows, providing a secured - yet unauthenticated - entry point to trigger processes and route data to specific Enterprise Clouds.

Flexibility for Cloud integrations

  • Make your Clouds communicate - without replicating all data
  • Centralize your integration policies
  • Unify a central - straightforward way to integrate Clouds
  • Process oriented - focus on the links, not on the destinations
  • Build a compute engine for your clouds
  • Centralize your integration process rules & documentation

Trusted Platform

  • Open-Source core platform & framework
  • Ecosystem of add-ons & partners
  • Enterprise premium support
  • 1 click deploy to your Heroku platform

Built for both Developers & Business

  • Developers: get the flexibility that Enterprise Clouds integration needs. Focus on the links, Skyflow provides the framework for authentications, security, a simple API (external events) and a large library of Enterprise Clouds methods.
  • Business: get a global vision of data flows with a user-friendly web interface where you’ll find events to flows mapping, flows documentations and rules, and provisioned add-ons.
